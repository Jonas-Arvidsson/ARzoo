About the Project

Enter Virtual Reality

One person steps in to the world of virtual reality and see through the eyes of NASA's own Mars Rover. The person who sees through the rovers cameras must tell Mission Control what obsticles lay ahead and how to avoid them in order to reach their destination and complete the mission.

Take Control

Another person is in charge of the Nasa's Mission Control and sends commands to the rover in order to control it. Mission Control has access to information about the rover such as current health status, battery status, and

Communicate

The rover and Mission Control have to constantly communicate in order to achieve their goal and make sure the rover survives the mission.
